Push-to-Kindle Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-26

Push-to-Kindle sends the page URL you choose to our backend service so the service can extract article content, create an EPUB file, and email it to your Kindle address.

What data is used

  • The page URL you explicitly send.
  • The Kindle email address you configure in the extension.
  • Operational logs from Google Cloud services (for reliability and abuse prevention).

What we do not do

  • We do not require user account sign-in.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not intentionally retain article content after delivery.

Third-party services

  • Google Cloud Run (API hosting)
  • Google Secret Manager (service secrets)
  • Gmail API (outbound delivery to Kindle)
  • Amazon Send-to-Kindle (final document delivery)

Contact

For questions, contact the extension publisher through the Chrome Web Store listing.
